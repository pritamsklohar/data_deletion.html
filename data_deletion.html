Data Deletion Instructions

How to Delete Your Data

To delete your data from Java Quiz App, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Java Quiz App
  2. Go to your Profile section
  3. Click on "Settings" or "Account Settings"
  4. Select "Delete Account" or "Delete Data"
  5. Confirm your decision

What Data Will Be Deleted

The following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your profile information
  • Quiz history and scores
  • Achievements and progress
  • Profile picture
  • Account preferences

Note: This action cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted.

Alternative Method

If you're unable to delete your data through the app, you can contact us directly:

Email: support@javaquizapp.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request

Please include your:

  • User ID or email address
  • Account creation date (if known)
  • Reason for deletion

Data Deletion Timeline

Once you request data deletion:

  • Your account will be deactivated immediately
  • Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete

Additional Information

If you have any questions about the data deletion process, please contact our support team at support@javaquizapp.com
